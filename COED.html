Leuven COED meetings


We regularly organize Leuven COED (Computing On Encrypted Data) meetings (LCM).
These meetings are inspired by similar meetings held in the London area, London-(ish) Lattice Coding and Crypto meetings, but on different topics. The first COED day will be held on Monday 14th of October at ESAT. See the website for more details.
