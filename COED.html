Leuven COED meetings


We are organizing the Leuven COED Meetings (LCM)

These meetings are inspired by similar meetings held in the London area, London-(ish) Lattice Coding and Crypto meetings, and are aimed at connecting researchers with a common interest in the broad research areas of secure multiparty computation and homomorphic encryptions, and their applications.

The (informal) meetings will consist of several talks on related topics, with the main goal of encouraging interaction and new collaborations among researchers from different institutions and companies.
